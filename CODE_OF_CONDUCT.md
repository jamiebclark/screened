# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors and maintainers of Screened are committed to making participation a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and code review
- Accept feedback graciously and give it kindly
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project

Unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a private GitHub issue or emailing the maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as a reference standard.
