# Watch Parties

Watch parties let you schedule a viewing session for a specific movie or TV show and invite friends from your Screened network. Everyone gets notified, can accept or decline, and the host can download a calendar invite for their records.

## How it works

1. **Find a title** — Navigate to any movie or TV show page.
2. **Schedule the party** — Click the **Watch Party** button. Pick a date and time, then select which friends to invite.
3. **Wait for RSVPs** — Invited friends see the pending invite on their Watch Parties page and can accept or decline. You'll get a notification when they respond.
4. **Watch together** — When the time comes, your party shows as Confirmed once guests have accepted.

## Creating a watch party

Watch parties are always tied to a specific title. To start one:

- Go to any movie or TV show page and click the **Watch Party** button
- From the **Movie Night Picker**, use the Watch Party button on any result card
- From your **Watch History** calendar view, use the Schedule Watch Party shortcut on a past entry

## Managing parties

The **Watch Parties** page shows:

- **Upcoming** — parties you're hosting or have been invited to that haven't happened yet
- **Past** — completed or cancelled parties

Click any party to open its detail page, where hosts can cancel the party, everyone can see who has responded, and you can download a `.ics` calendar file.

## Notifications

Invitees receive an in-app notification when you schedule a party. Hosts are notified when a guest responds. If Discord DM notifications are enabled, those fire as well.
